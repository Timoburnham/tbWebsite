Timo Burnham has done a wide variety of jobs in his life, from things as different as night security, game tester and acrobatics instructor. From these jobs he's gathered plenty of fascinating material to use in his books.

He enjoys both reading and writing fast-paced stories with lots of action, adventure and mystery as well as some drama and romance on the side. Maybe one day he will be able to sit still long enough to write one of those so called "epics", but until then his stories' are lean, mean and straight to the point.
